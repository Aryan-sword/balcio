The Balcão Legacy

The heart of every home – a place for children and pets to play – neighbours, friends and families to catch up after a long day. A place that makes you truly feel at home. Allowing you to unwind, put up your feet, curl up with a book, listen to music, and soak in the beauty of Goa. A place to celebrate traditions and festivals, a place to celebrate life.

Distinctive in Goan architecture, the Balcão features bright, hand-cast concrete seats called sopos, providing a friendly spot for locals and passersby to enjoy a chat over a hot cup of chão. Framed by thick curved walls, graceful stairs, and pillars that uphold a traditional roof, the Balcão is more than just an entrance – it’s a symbol of community and connection. Originally inspired by European designs, the Balcão has evolved to suit Goa’s unique climate, becoming an integral part of the region's cultural landscape and a status symbol in Goan homes.

The Balcão welcomed us to immerse into Goa’s culture and build a community, inspiring us to invite others to experience Goa in its truest form. With Balcão Homes, we want every homeowner to unlock their unique fingerprint in Goa while soaking in the rich legacy that Goa has to offer.
